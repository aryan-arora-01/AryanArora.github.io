Research Interests

My research interests include Labor Economics, Public Policy, Urban Economics, and Industrial Organization. I am particularly focused on low-income housing policies and their impact on community welfare.

Current Projects

I am currently working on several projects that analyze the effects of housing policies on economic mobility and access to resources for low-income families. I am also studying the relationship between urban development and labor market outcomes.
