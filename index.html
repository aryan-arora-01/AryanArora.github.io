Aryan Arora

Research / CV / Reading Group


Welcome! I am Pre-doctoral Fellow at Yale working with Professors Winnie van Dijk and Scott Nelson on topics related to low-income housing policies. My research interests span Labor, Public, Urban Economics, and IO.


Previously I worked for Professor Don Moore at UC Berkeley and Dr. David Wiczer at the Federal Reserve Bank of Atlanta. I also had brief stints at the Federal Reserve Bank of Boston, the Business for a Better World Center at George Mason University, and the Center for Emotional Intelligence at Yale.


I received my undergraduate degree in Mathematics and Economics from Carleton College.


I also lead the Yale Predoc Reading Group. My email address is aryan.arora@yale.edu.
